Aggregate Driveways


Aggregate driveways offer a distinctive blend of rustic appeal and useful benefits, making them an progressively popular choice for homeowners across State. These adaptable surfaces not only boost the look of rural properties but also provide a budget-friendly and minimal upkeep solution for your driveway needs.

Maintenance Requirements

While gravel driveways are low-maintenance, they do require some maintenance to keep them in top condition:

  • Regular Raking: Occasional raking helps maintain an level surface and prevents tracks from forming.
  • Weed Control: Apply a weed barrier during installation and remove any weeds that may appear over time.
  • Topping Up: Add a additional layer of gravel every few years to maintain the optimal depth and appearance.
  • Snow Removal: Use care when clearing snow to avoid scattering the gravel.
